Common Dishwasher Problems and Faults That We Fix

1
Dishwasher Not Draining

Water pooling at the bottom of your dishwasher is often caused by clogged filters, blocked drain hoses, or faulty drain pumps. We clean drainage systems, replace damaged pumps, and fix blockages to restore proper water flow and prevent standing water issues.

2
Dishwasher Not Cleaning Properly

Dishes coming out dirty or with residue indicates problems with spray arms, water inlet valves, or detergent dispensers. Our technicians inspect and clean spray arm nozzles, replace faulty valves, calibrate water temperature, and ensure optimal cleaning performance for spotless dishes every time.

3
Water Leakage Issues

Leaking dishwashers can damage your kitchen floor and cabinets. We identify leak sources including worn door seals, cracked hoses, loose connections, or damaged tub gaskets. Our experts replace seals, tighten connections, and repair any component causing water to escape from your dishwasher unit.

4
Dishwasher Won't Start

When your dishwasher refuses to start, the problem could be electrical faults, door latch failures, control panel issues, or thermal fuses. We diagnose power supply problems, replace faulty door switches, repair control boards, and ensure all safety mechanisms function correctly for reliable operation.

5
Strange Noises During Operation

Unusual grinding, buzzing, or rattling sounds indicate worn pump bearings, damaged wash motor, loose spray arms, or foreign objects in the system. Our technicians thoroughly inspect internal components, remove obstructions, lubricate moving parts, and replace worn components to restore quiet, smooth dishwasher operation.

6
Door Latch and Seal Problems

A dishwasher door that won't close properly or lock securely prevents operation and can cause leaks. We repair or replace broken door latches, hinges, springs, and gaskets. Our service ensures your dishwasher door closes tightly, maintains proper seal integrity, and operates safely throughout each wash cycle.
